About David

As a veteran business journalist, David Morrill has spent much of his professional career seeking the common threads that make businesses successful. His media stints include the Bay Area News Group which includes the Oakland Tribune, Contra Costa Times, San Jose Mercury, Chicago Tribune, and The Denver Post. Over the course of his career he has written more than 5,000 articles with the majority focused on small business issues.
